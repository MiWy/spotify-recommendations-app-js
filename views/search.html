How to

SpotiBar allows you to search Spotify recommendations based on several criteria. In order to use it, you should make sure that:

  1. You are signed in to your Spotify account. To do so, click the button in the top-right corner
  2. You've selected at least 1 and not more than 5 seeds (artists/tracks/genres). You can search for them by quering field on the left
  3. (optional) You can also set optional criteria, such as Duration, Key and others to get more specific recommendations

Some optional criteria have ranges given in placeholders. Search mechanisms will ignore such criteria that are not within range.
